SUI

Subtract immediate to accumulator


Subtracts from accumulator one byte of immediate data.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
SUI d8 0xD6 2 7

*d8 means 8 bits of data.

Example

MVI
A,05
SUI
03

After executing these instructions accumulator will contain 0x02.
