DCR

Decrement register or memory


Contents of the register or memory are decremented by 1.

Condition bits affected: Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
DCR B 0x05 1 5
DCR D 0x15 1 5
DCR H 0x25 1 5
DCR M 0x35 1 10
DCR C 0x0D 1 5
DCR E 0x1D 1 5
DCR L 0x2D 1 5
DCR A 0x3D 1 5

Example

MVI
B,05
DCR
B

After executing these instructions register B will contain 0x04.
