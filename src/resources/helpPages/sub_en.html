SUB

Subtract register or memory from accumulator


Sbutracts from accumulator contents of register or memory.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
SUB B 0x90 1 4
SUB C 0x91 1 4
SUB D 0x92 1 4
SUB E 0x93 1 4
SUB H 0x94 1 4
SUB L 0x95 1 4
SUB M 0x96 1 7
SUB A 0x97 1 4

Example

MVI
A,F2
MVI
B,04
SUB
B

After executing these instructions accumulator will contain 0xEE.
