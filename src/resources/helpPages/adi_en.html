ADI

Add immediate to accumulator


Adds to contents of accumulator 8 bits of immediate data.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry

Instruction Hex code Lenght in bytes Length in cycles
ADI d8 0xC6 2 7

*d8 means 8 bits of data.

Example

MVI
A,05
ADI
03

After executing these instructions the content of the accumulator will be 0x08.
