INX

Increment register pair


Contents of the pair of registers are incremented by 1.

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
INX B 0x03 1 5
INX D 0x13 1 5
INX H 0x23 1 5
INX SP 0x33 1 5

Example

MVI
B,00
MVI
C,02
INX
B

After executing these istructions registers B and C will contain 0x00, and 0x03.
