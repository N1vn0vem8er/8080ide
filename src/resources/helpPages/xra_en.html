XRA

Logical XOR register or memory with accumulator


Logical XOR of accumulator and register or memory. Carry bit is always set to 0.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Zero, Sign, Parity.

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
XRA B 0xA8 1 4
XRA C 0xA9 1 4
XRA D 0xAA 1 4
XRA E 0xAB 1 4
XRA H 0xAC 1 4
XRA L 0xAD 1 4
XRA M 0xAE 1 7
XRA A 0xAF 1 4

Example

MVI
A,F2
MVI
B,04
XRA
B

After executing these instructions accumulator will contain 0xF6.
