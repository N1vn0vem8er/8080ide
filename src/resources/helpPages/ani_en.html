ANI

And immediate with accumulator


Logical AND on accumulator and immediate data after instruction code. Carry is always set to 0.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Sign, Zero, Parity

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Lenght in cycles
ANI d8 0xE6 2 7

*d8 means 8 bits of data.

Example

MVI
A,05
ANI
03

After this instructions content of accumulator will be 0x01.
