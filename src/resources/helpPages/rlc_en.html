RLC

Rotate accumulator left


Contents of accumulator are rotated one bit left. Most important bit sets Carry and it is beeing transfered to least important position.

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
RLC 0x07 1 4

Example

MVI
A,F0
RLC

After executing these instructions register A will contain 0xE1 and Carry will be set to 1.
