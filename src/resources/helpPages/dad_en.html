DAD

Double add


16-bits from register pair are added to 16-bits stored in registers H and L. Resoult is stored in H and L.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry.

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles Description
DAD B 0x09 1 10 B and C are added to H and L.
DAD D 0x19 1 10 D and E are added to H and L.
DAD H 0x29 1 10 H and L are added to H and L.
DAD SP 0x39 1 10 SP is added to H and L.

Example

MVI
H,A1
MVI
L,7B
MVI
B,33
MVI
C,9F
DAD
B

After executing those instructions H and L will contain 0xD5, 0x1A.
