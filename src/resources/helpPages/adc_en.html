ADC

ADD register or memory to accumulator with carry


Adds to contents of accumulator the contents of register or memory plus the contents of the Carry.

Instruction Hex code Lenght in bytes Length in cycles
ADC B 0x88 1 4
ADC C 0x89 1 4
ADC D 0x8A 1 4
ADC E 0x8B 1 4
ADC H 0x8C 1 4
ADC L 0x8D 1 4
ADC M 0x8E 1 7
ADC A 0x8F 1 4

Example

MVI
A,F2
MVI
B,04
ADC
B

If Carry = 1, after executing these instructions the content of the accumulator will be 0xF7.
