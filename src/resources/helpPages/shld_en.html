SHLD

Store H and L direct


Contents of registers H and L are seved under memory address.

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
SHLD a16 0x22 3 16

*a16 means 16 bits of address.

Example

MVI
H,AB
MVI
L,10
SHLD
0012

After executing these instructions contents of register H will be saved in memory under address 0x0013 and register L under 0x0012.

Before SHLD Address After SHLD
0x00 0x0012 0x10
0x00 0x0013 0xAB
