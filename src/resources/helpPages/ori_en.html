ORI

Or immediate with accumulator


Logical OR on accumulator and immediate data.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
ORI d8 0xF6 2 7

*d8 means 8 bits of data.

Example

MVI
A,05
ORI
03

After executing these instructions accumulator will contain 0x07.
