SBB

Subtract register or memory from accumulator with borrow


Substracts from accumulator register or memory with Carry.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
SBB B 0x98 1 4
SBB C 0x99 1 4
SBB D 0x9A 1 4
SBB E 0x9B 1 4
SBB H 0x9C 1 4
SBB L 0x9D 1 4
SBB M 0x9E 1 7
SBB A 0x9F 1 4

Example

MVI
A,F2
MVI
B,04
SBB
B

If Carry = 1 after executing these instructions accumulator will contain 0xED.
