ACI

Add immediate to accumulator with Carry


Adds to contents of accumulator 8 bits of immediate data plus the contents of the Carry.

Condition bits affected: Carry, Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry

Instruction Hex code Lenght in bytes Length in cycles
ACI d8 0xCE 2 7

*d8 means 8 bits of data.

Example

MVI
A,05
ACI
03

If Carry = 1, after executing these instructions the content of the accumulator will be 0x09
