INR

Increment register or memory


Contents of the register or memory are incremented by 1.

Condition bits affected: Sign, Zero, Parity, Auxiliary Carry

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
INR B 0x04 1 5
INR D 0x14 1 5
INR H 0x24 1 5
INR M 0x34 1 10
INR C 0x0C 1 5
INR E 0x1C 1 5
INR L 0x2C 1 5
INR A 0x3C 1 5

Example

MVI
B,00
INR
B

After executing these instructions register B will contain 0x01.
