PCHL

Load program counter


Jump instruction to memory addres indicated by contents of registers H and L. Contents of register H replace 8 most important bits of program counter PC, contents of register L 8 least important bits of PC.

Instruction Hex code Length in bytes Length in cycles
PCHL 0xE9 1 10

Example

MVI
H,00
MVI
L,2A
PCHL

After executing these instructions program will jump to memory address 0x002A.
